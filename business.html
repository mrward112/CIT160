Hilltop Farms Catering

So? What stage is this business in?

This business has gone through multiple names and phases. Orginally it was called Kindred Spirits Catering before we moved to Idaho. Most recently a concept of a larger scale business has been thought of that would incorporate more than just food. Who knows what the future of this business idea will become?
